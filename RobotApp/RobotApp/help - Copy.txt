-List of Commands-

PLACE x,y,facing	Place the robot in 'x','y' coordinate 'facing' in certain direction (north,south,east,west)
REPORT			Displays the current location and orientation of the robot
MOVE			Robot will step forward depending on where it is facing
LEFT			Rotates the robot to its left
RIGHT			Rotates the robot to its right

-End of Commands-

Additional Info:
 Table where the robot is place have only 5x5 tiles
 Movement or placement that will make the robot fall will be ignored
 Invalid commands will be ignored
 Movement commands (MOVE, LEFT, and RIGHT) will be ignored if no robot is placed in the table
